Basic Rules

3-player game using 52 cards. Each player gets 16 cards, 4 cards are reserved. The Landlord is decided through bidding and receives the reserve cards (20 total). The other two Farmers team up against the Landlord.

Bidding

A random player starts bidding (1/2/3 points), taking turns. You can bid higher or pass. Bidding 3 instantly becomes Landlord, otherwise the highest bidder wins. If no one bids, cards are redealt.

Play Order

The Landlord plays first, turns go counter-clockwise. You are at the bottom, right is next player, left is previous player.
